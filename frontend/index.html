What it does

Everything you need to
understand your data

No notebooks. No setup. Just upload and get answers.

AI Executive Summary

Groq LLM reads your dataset and writes a plain-English summary of what your data means, key trends, and what to watch out for.

Auto Visualizations

Histograms, category breakdowns, correlation heatmaps, and scatter plots — auto-generated for every column with zero configuration.

Statistical Analysis

Mean, median, mode, std deviation, quartiles, outlier detection, and missing value analysis — calculated for every numeric column.

AI Insights Cards

Correlation insights, data quality flags, trend detection, and ML readiness scores — surfaced automatically as scannable insight cards.

ML Recommendations

Automatically suggests the best ML task type (classification, regression, clustering), target column, and top model candidates for your data.

Ask Your Dataset

Chat directly with your data. Ask "What's the average revenue by region?" or "Which column has the most outliers?" and get instant AI answers.

Multi-format File Support

Upload CSV, Excel (.xlsx / .xls), or JSON files. PapaParse handles malformed CSVs gracefully, with configurable delimiter detection.

Groq-Powered Speed

Groq's ultra-fast LLM inference means summaries and insights stream back in seconds, not minutes. Bring your own API key — free tier works fine.

Chart.js Visualizations

Histograms, bar charts, scatter plots, and a correlation heatmap powered by Chart.js and the matrix plugin — fully interactive and zoomable.

Export Everything

100% Client-Side Privacy

Your data never leaves your browser except for the AI summary calls (which only send column names and basic statistics to Groq — never raw rows). Your API key is stored in localStorage only.
